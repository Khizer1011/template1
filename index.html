Our Story

Built on a belief that great spaces change lives.

Founded in 2006 by structural engineer Marcus Cole and interior architect Elena Vasquez, ARCHITECT was born from a shared conviction: that the boundary between engineering and design is a false one. The most enduring spaces are those where both disciplines speak as one.

Over eighteen years, we have delivered more than 120 projects across nine countries — from private residences and commercial headquarters to cultural institutions and adaptive reuse projects. Each bears the hallmarks of our integrated approach: structural boldness, material integrity, and spaces that resonate long after the scaffolding comes down.

Our Values

We tell clients what their project truly requires — not what they want to hear. Structural reality is non-negotiable; aesthetic ambition is unlimited within it.

We source materials with provenance — reclaimed oak, hand-pressed concrete, recycled steel. Every surface tells a story before a single person occupies the space.

A project doesn't end at handover. We offer post-occupancy reviews, maintenance guidance, and ongoing consultancy for all completed builds.
